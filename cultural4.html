For example, Sinead O’Connor, a popular Irish song-writer and singer, wrote a very strongly worded letter to Miley Cyrus about her recent activities in the public eye. She is not happy with the way young girls are learning from stars in the entertainment business. “Nothing but harm will come in the long run, from allowing yourself to be exploited, and it is absolutely NOT in ANY way an empowerment of yourself or any other young women, for you to send across the message that you are to be valued (even by you) more for your sexual appeal than your obvious talent.” Sinead agrees that the message given across is not proper or empowering to the women of 2013.
