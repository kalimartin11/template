Paragraph 7

Ultimately, it is important to regain what we’ve lost and to treat women once again with loving care and respect. There are a lot of people in the world that hate seeing the way women are portrayed today and to how low of standard we have fallen. We treat each other with less care and value. There needs to be a change now. We first must change our attitudes and know that we deserve better and our actions should express that. As women we set a standard in the world as mothers, sisters, and friends. We are the caregivers, the heart of the home, the emotional beings that make the world a better place to live. Instead of our society heading in a downward spiral, we need to start moving back up toward a more respectable place in society. One day women will be held on a pedestal, but first we must know that we are truly worthy.
