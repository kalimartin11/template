Paragraph 7

Without packaging we wouldn’t be living so comfortably today. There are places in the world that aren’t getting the nutrition and food they need. Packaging helps ship packages of food to these places where they try to save lives. It is hard to understand the importance of packaging, but it is crucial. Packaging will always be a vital part of the world and will never go away. Michigan State University provides the award winning School of Packaging to anyone interested in Packaging. There they will read, write, and research everything there is to learn about the industry. As packaging increases in popularity, Michigan State will produce more incredible packagers into the industry.
